Final paper

My final paper in high school was about diving deeper into Freud's theory of personality.Freud proposed that the mind is divided into three components: id, ego, and superego, and that the interactions and conflicts among the components create personality. My mentor was psychology/philosophy professor Nermin Salkić.

Zaboravljeni genije "Nikola Tesla"

As part of the mandatory projects during high school, I was part of a work group that researched Tesla's life and the importance of his work for the entire population. That's why we visited Belgrade and the Nikola Tesla Museum, and all the information we collected was presented through the show in order to more easily show and approximate the importance of this topic. This project brought me closer to methods of doing scientific researches.

"Cifre i riječi"

One of the most interesting projects during my studies at @pmf was a project while taking the course "Machine learning". The name of the project is numbers and words, and the goal was to make a python program that will expect from user audio record and then print out spoken words. The user is limited of saying just numbers from 0 to 9. During work on this project, I have learned more about neural networks, and the process of preparing data that includes auto augmentation, spectrograms, etc.
